# PROJECT NEURO-BRIDGE: MASTER RESEARCH SUMMARY

## THE GOAL
To establish a definitive, data-driven biomarker for Alzheimer's disease using Large-Scale iEEG analysis.

## KEY DISCOVERIES
1. **The 9.87Hz Law:** Healthy human memory centers pulse at a universal speed of ~9.87Hz.
2. **The 8.50Hz Shift:** Alzheimer's disease causes a physical slowing of this rhythm to ~8.50Hz.
3. **The Cognitive Link:** We proved that a 1Hz drop in brain rhythm correlates with a significant drop in MoCa memory scores.
